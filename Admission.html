Admission & Requirements

We are excited to welcome new learners to our growing BVBC family!
Below are the details you need to get started on your enrollment journey with us.

Enrollment Process

  1. Incquire - Visit our campus or contact our office to begin the admission process.

  2. Submit Requirements - Provide the required documents for evaluation.

  3. Student Assessment - Applicants may be asked to complete an academic readiness test or attend an interview.

  4. Confirmation & Payment - Once accepted, complete payment and attent the orientation session.

Age Eligibility

  • Kindergarten - Must be 5 years old by October 31of the current school year.

  • Elementary - Must be 6 years old by October 31of the current school year.
