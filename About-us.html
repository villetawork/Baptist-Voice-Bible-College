Baptist Voice Bible College, also known as BVBC, is a private school that offers foundational and higher education. It has been operated and managed by Grace Baptist Church since 2000. The Basic Education Department utilizes the Accelerated Christian Education system that was started in the early 1970s by dedicated parents who desired their children reared under the teaching of God's word - the Bible. A.C.E. penetrated over 130 countries, where parents desire their children's lives patterned after the timeless principles of God's Word.

Department of Education-Region IV-A has given full recognition to BVBC: Junior High School (years 7-10) since 2023, Elementary (Grades 1 through 6) since 2017, and Pre-Elementary (Kindergarten ) since 2016, as well as a permit to operate the Senior High School (Academic Track) since 2018.

On the other hand, the College Department offers a Bachelor in Theology and a Bachelor of Science in Christian Education. The commitment of dedicated staff and diligent students in these departments has bridged the Licensure Examination for Teachers with 100% marks for three consecutive undertakings.

Message from the President

Dear Friends and Visitors,

Greetings in the matchless name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

It is with sincere gratitude and heartfelt joy that I welcome you to the official website of Baptist Voice Bible College. We count it a great privilege to introduce you to the vision, values, and ministry of this institution, which exists solely for the glory of God and the advancement of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Founded upon the timeless truths of the Word of God and anchored in the historic Baptist faith, our college is committed to the training and preparation of dedicated men and women for faithful service in local churches, mission fields, and Christian ministries worldwide. As an Independent Baptist institution, we uphold the authority of Scripture, the autonomy of the local church, and the centrality of the Gospel in all that we do.

At BVBC, students are challenged academically, enriched spiritually, and equipped practically for the work of the ministry. Our programs are intentionally designed to develop biblically sound, spiritually mature, and ministry-ready servants of Christ. Through sound doctrinal instruction, fervent prayer, and real-world ministry experience, we aim to cultivate leaders who will remain steadfast in truth and faithful in service.

This website serves as a window into the life and labor of our college. Whether you are seeking a place to prepare for God's call upon your life, exploring opportunities to partner with us in prayer and support, or simply learning more about our mission, we are honored by your interest. I encourage you to explore the resources available here and to contact us should you desire further information or assistance.

On behalf of our faculty, staff, and student body, thank you for visiting. May the Lord richly bless you as you seek His will and serve His Kingdom.

For His Glory,
Rudy Calayan
BVBC - President
